Whale Behaviours

Some records of whale sightings include descriptions of whale activities such as breaching, tail slapping, or feeding. Most entries, however, do not include detailed behavioural notes.

Sea Surface Temperature Trends

Sea Surface Temperatures show a slight increasing trend since 1980, suggesting warming ocean conditions that may affect whale migration patterns and food availability.
